Samantha Rose is a transgender woman game developer. She has made all kinds of games, with people of all kinds of backgrounds, both in games and out. She prides herself as a collaborator above all else. She sees her role as a developer to enable the rest of the team to make the best game possible. She wants them to have the best systems, and the best tools possible. She focuses not only on creating robust, stable, and readable code but on making sure that code fits the needs of those who will be working with it. She has been working in C# and Unity for the last 6 years, the last 3 of which were in a professional capacity. She believes the key to success is communication, empathy, and respect between all team members.
